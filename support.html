General

Synaps is a sound frequency wellness app that uses scientifically backed binaural beats, solfeggio frequencies, nature sounds, and ambient soundscapes to help you focus, relax, sleep, and meditate. It also includes breathing exercises, a focus timer, and an adaptive sound engine that responds to weather and motion.
Binaural beats are created when two slightly different frequencies are played in each ear. Your brain perceives a third "beat" at the difference between the two frequencies. For example, if 200 Hz is played in one ear and 210 Hz in the other, your brain perceives a 10 Hz beat — which corresponds to the Alpha brainwave range associated with relaxation. Different frequency ranges promote different mental states: Delta (1-4 Hz) for deep sleep, Theta (4-8 Hz) for meditation, Alpha (8-14 Hz) for relaxation, and Beta (14-30 Hz) for focus.
Solfeggio frequencies are a set of specific tones historically associated with healing and wellness practices. Common solfeggio frequencies include 396 Hz (liberation from fear), 432 Hz (natural harmony), 528 Hz (transformation and DNA repair), and 741 Hz (awakening intuition). While scientific evidence is limited, many users report positive subjective experiences.
Headphones are required for binaural beats to work, as each ear needs to receive a slightly different frequency. For ambient sounds, nature soundscapes, and solfeggio frequencies, speakers work fine — but headphones always provide the best experience.

Features

The Frequency Tuner lets you create custom pure tones and binaural beats. Select specific frequencies from Sub-Bass (20-60 Hz) to High-Mid (800-1000 Hz), choose a waveform (sine, triangle, square, sawtooth), and save your favorite presets for quick access.
Synaps offers guided breathing techniques including Box Breathing (4-4-4-4), the 4-7-8 method for sleep, and the Wim Hof technique. Each exercise includes visual guides and optional haptic cues.
With your permission, Synaps can adapt sounds based on your local weather (rain, temperature) and physical activity (stationary, walking, running). This creates a more immersive, context-aware listening experience. You can enable or disable this in Settings.
The Ambient Mixer lets you layer up to 5 ambient sounds (rain, ocean, wind, forest, fire, etc.) on top of any playing soundscape. Each layer has its own volume control, so you can create your perfect sound environment.
Previously listened soundscapes may be cached for offline use. The Frequency Tuner generates audio on-device and always works offline. However, you need an internet connection to browse new content, sync your account, and manage your subscription.

Troubleshooting

Make sure your device is not in silent mode and the volume is turned up. Check that no other audio apps are playing. Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, go to Settings → Audio Cache → Clear to reset cached audio data.
Binaural beats require stereo headphones. Make sure you're using headphones (not a single speaker or mono earpiece) and that the left and right channels are correctly oriented. The effect is subtle — give it at least 10-15 minutes for your brain to entrain to the frequency.
Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem continues, ensure you have the latest version installed from the App Store or Google Play. As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app (your account data will be preserved if you log in again).
